PINEAPPLE PILOT FOR SPACESHIP EARTH

Description

You have made it clear on several occasions, Bucy, that you are not expecially fond of puns. Maybe it is because they were offered plain. Possibly you will change your mind after trying this punapple pie.

Ingredients

  • 1 stick butter
  • 2/3 cup sugar
  • 1 lb. crushed pineapple with juice
  • 1/8 tsp. salt
  • 3 egg whites
  • 4 cup sliced almonds
  • 3 egg yolks

Steps

  1. Mix butter, sugar, and egg yolks, and beat until creamy.
  2. Add crushed pineapple and sliced almonds to mixture.
  3. Beat egg whites with salt and fold into rest of ingredients.
  4. Top with crushed vanilla wafers and chill for 12 hours before searving.
